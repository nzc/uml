Research Direction：

Tropical disease diagnosis and pathogen identification technology development and application research, tropical pathogen and vector-based drug resistance mechanism research, development and application of tropical disease vaccine research, tropical disease-related Peptide drug development and application research, pathology and diagnosis of tropical diseases and prevention and control measures, prevention and control of tropical diseases, the study of high incidence of Li disease in our province and the relationship between tropical diseases.

Task：

To carry out the parasite, zygomatic rickettsia and other pathogenic molecular medical research. Its main task is to use the known gene sequences and bioinformatics methods in Genbank to screen possible target genes and identify their functions, to master the pathogen in the environment and drug selection pressure gene variation, pathogens and host (human and savings (Molecular mechanism of pathogenesis and immunity), laying a solid foundation for the development and research of new therapeutic drugs, diagnostic reagents and gene vaccines.

Tropical disease diagnosis and pathogen identification technology is the basis of tropical disease prevention and treatment technology, clinical diagnosis, efficacy and epidemiological investigation have an important role. In addition to mastering and utilizing the existing basic diagnosis and pathogen identification techniques of tropical diseases, the Key Laboratory of Tropical Diseases should continue to track the progress of research at home and abroad, and continuously apply new technologies to basic research , And actively explore the feasibility of its clinical application. It will focus on molecular biology, genomics, proteomics, immunology, cell biology and modern tissue (immune) pathology techniques to find new targets for the diagnosis and treatment of tropical diseases, and strive to research and develop with independent knowledge Property rights of tropical disease diagnosis and pathogen identification technology, and these techniques applied to clinical and epidemiological studies. And strive to build a laboratory with the discovery, diagnosis and identification of new tropical pathogen capacity of the domestic authority of the laboratory.

Resistance of tropical pathogens to drug resistance and vector-borne resistance to various insecticides is another major factor in the control of tropical diseases such as malaria. In this research field, the laboratory will mainly apply molecular biology, genomics, molecular immunology methods and means to study molecular mechanisms of tropical disease pathogens and vector-based drug resistance mechanism, and then find the treatment and intervention of tropical pathogens And insect-borne vector resistance of the new method.

The current researchers generally believe that the vaccine is the most safe, cheap and effective means of prevention and treatment of tropical diseases. But today, in the field of tropical disease vaccine, especially malaria vaccine research, although the international community has invested a lot of manpower, financial resources and material resources, scientists have done a lot of research work, has not yet developed and produced any clear clinical control effect Of the vaccine. Therefore, there are many research gaps in the development of tropical disease vaccine. Key Laboratory of Tropical Pathogen Biology will make full use of molecular biology, immunology, cell biology and contemporary advanced biotechnology methods and means, in addition to looking for effective induction of the host immune system of tropical disease pathogens and humoral cellular immune response vector and Zuo , To solve the traditional vaccine immunization titer is too low, the duration is not long traditional vaccine strategy deficiencies, but also the use of new construction and preparation of vaccine technology and strategies focus on the development of malaria, dengue fever, Rickettsia and other tropical diseases Prevention and treatment of vaccine.

Polypeptides are biologically active substances involved in various cellular functions in organisms. Almost all cells are regulated by peptides, which are involved in various fields such as hormone secretion, nerve, cell growth and reproduction. The study of the effects of peptides secreted by the body on the pathogenesis and prognosis of the disease will not only contribute to the understanding of human physiological and pathophysiological processes, but also provide a theoretical basis for clinical diagnosis and treatment of diseases, but also help to find some New proteins associated with disease. The Key Laboratory of Tropical Diseases will focus on molecular and cellular biology to search for active peptides from marine organisms and tropical pathogens. It will seek to discover several polypeptides with tropical disease activity, and explore the role of these peptides in Tropical disease incidence and prognosis of the mechanism of action for the prevention and treatment of tropical diseases to find new methods and strategies.
